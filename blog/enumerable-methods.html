[Introduction to the Ruby Enumerable#map Method]

December 10, 2015

The Enumerable module in Ruby allows collection objects to access a subset of behaviors and methods defined in terms of its each method. The each will outline how an object iterates through its elements. Once the each method is implemented, th Enumerable module is able to provide a wide range on methods onto the collection object.
